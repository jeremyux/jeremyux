JEREMY LENGUYEN

Queen of Raw Homepage

UI Designer and Web Developer

Queen of Raw is an up and coming fashion textile and material startup. The company's mission statement is to bring fair-trade, sustainable, and artisan-sourced materials to its e-commerce platform. Queen of Raw came to me with the job of redesigning their website's front page to showcase their social media endeavors as well as promote new products.

Hero Section
The purpose of this section is to keep users updated with things like new blog posts or Youtube videos. This section should also list all of the possible categories Queen of Raw has to offer.

Promo Sales Section
Queen of Raw works with independant artists and suppliers. They also like to promote those partners' special offers when possible. This section makes it easy for the company to quickly showcase what is being promoted or is on sale.

Content Channels
One of the asks was to surface all of the great content that Queen of Raw puts out. I wanted this area to be the "Hub" where people can discover the many cool things Queen of Raw is doing to bring sustainability to fashion.

Social
Lastly this section is to show a grid of Instagram posts. Additionally this is an opportunity for Queen of Raw to promote favorite offers of the week. Much like a candy-rack at a grocery store it is the last thing you see before you reach the end of the page.
