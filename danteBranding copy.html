UX Designer

Dante Cuddy is a probate lawyer based in the bay area that wanted a revamp his branding and logo. Services he provides require trust and security on the clients part because in the end they trust he would help them in the afterlife. I wanted a facade that conveyed that.

Branding
My approach with this project is to bring a little comteporary flavor into the usual law field. I wanted to step away fron the gavels and the scales and really just delve in deeper to what Dante really stands for in his own words: stability, dependability and balance. The cairn is a symbol of guidance for hikers lost on their trail. In the ambiguous world of probate lat Dante would be the guiding light for his clients.

Design Decisions
As for the type I chose a chose a font combination of Cinzel and Poiret One. I wanted the type to convey a law office yet contemperary enough to bring some modern flavor to the logo. I took this dark green color from the 1st iteration and brought it closer to the organic spectrum like the stone motif I chose:

Primary - Pantone 7699 C

Secondary - Pantone 5405 C

Visualization
Finally I wanted Dante to really visualize seeing the different applications his new branding can have so that he can really understand the impact something like a brand change can have. Once he was finally onboard with this concept I wanted to tackle the vision of his online precense which you can see here.
